About Vukasin

Vukasin Stancevic is a Web designer delving into fields of Photography, Typography, Game design, UI Design and Product design. He was born in Belgrade, Serbia. Strong arts and design professional with a Master's degree focused in Photography from Academy of Applied Arts Belgrade Serbia. He has worked in many places as a graphic designer and is currently working at Peer as a full-time product designer since October 2023.

Architecture

This visual piece by Vukasin Stancevic reflects a contemporary and bold approach to magazine design. The composition uses stark monochromatic tones to highlight the fluidity and dynamic forms inherent in modern structures.It resonates with the architectural theme, intertwining the precision of clean typography with the organic curves of the building. Convergence

Here we have a design that's both minimalistic and expressive, created by Vukasin Stancevic. It showcases the intersection of various creative disciplines, from architecture to package design, with a focus on industrial design. The grey tones and bold, overlaid text emphasize the sophistication and depth of these fields.

Pixelography

In this piece, Vukasin Stancevic offers a textured, digital mosaic that spotlights the work of artist Jun Seo Hahm. The pixelated portrait and typographic treatment suggest a blend of technology and artistry, fitting for Hahm's focus on digital expressions of biology-inspired life forms.
